About Synthetic Biology

Although our school does not have a special synthetic biology course, our team has organized new members to systematically study the synthetic biology textbook edited by Mr. Li Chun. In addition, we also organized the team members to learn the content in the handbook on the iGEM official website so that they can better understand the game and synthetic biology.

Team Calendar

April 6, 2021
The team is formed and brainstorming begins. Each member of the experimental group can conceive a topic that interests him, or collaborate with other team members to conceive a topic.
May 6, 2021
The instructor listened to each member's report, and then chose Qinyu Chan's project. After that, the team began to focus on the problems in Qinyu Chan's project.
September 1, 2021
After the approval of the college leaders and instructors, formally enter the laboratory and start the experiment.
