2、Project Modeling

https://2021.igem.org/Team:FAFU-CHINA/Model

First, we decompose the MVA pathway into a single elementary reaction according to the kinetic-based biochemical reaction model and construct a series of constant-coefficient differential equations to predict the relationship between the production of each aroma substance in a single cell and time. Secondly, we established an oscillator model to study the steady-state process of the microbial cell separation cycle. Use the response surface model based on BP neural network to explore and optimize the conditions of the co-cultivation system for the stable existence of E. coli and Saccharomyces cerevisiae, and help the Tongji_China team to support this model. Based on the conformational changes of Jα and helix in LOV2 under light and dark conditions, we use the signal node network model, use the BE-ABC optimization algorithm, and the Alphafold2 deep learning algorithm to predict the structure of the protein, and a series of related bioinformatics analysis. Finally, we also performed physical and mathematical simulations of the aroma emitted by the night light.

3、Proof of Concept

https://2021.igem.org/Team:FAFU-CHINA/Proof_Of_Concept

Our products do not require aseptic operations to mix the bacteria with the culture medium to start the fermentation system. On the other hand, our equipment is very simple, only need night light and fermentation tank to achieve all the desired functions. Our philosophy is to reduce complexity to simplicity, not to make buyers think they are buying large-scale laboratory equipment. This design makes the transportation and use of the product very convenient. Due to the cover made of the filter membrane, the microorganisms in the fermentor will not leak from the fermentor to the environment, and the external microorganisms will not enter the fermentor, thus avoiding the use of antibiotics and strains with resistance genes. Nowadays, aromatherapy has entered the lives of the general public, hoping to bring a new and different experience to those who love aromatherapy.
