3.2 Agarose gel electrophoresis
3.2.1 To separate medium size fragments, the agarose gel is used in a concentration of 1% w/v. Weigh
0.25/0.5/1g of agarose powder in an erlenmeyer flask.
3.2.2 Measure 25/50/100 mL of TBE buffer and add it to the erlenmeyer.
3.2.3 Put the mix in the microwave until the agarose is completely dissolved.
3.2.4 Add the gel nucleic stain(Fluorescein) by 0.01%.
3.2.5 Let the mix cool down till it can be added to the electrophoresis tray.
3.2.6 When agarose gel is solidified, remove the comb and put the gel into the Electrophoresis Cells
filled with TBE buffer.
3.2.7 Add 6X purple loading buffer mixed each DNA sample.
3.2.8 Load the DNA samples in the remaining wells(1.5μL for detection, all for purification).
3.2.9 Run the gel with the voltage 200V for 15 min.
3.2.10 Visualize the DNA fragments using a UV trans illuminator.

3.5 E.coli transformation
 Material:DH5α(competentcell）
3.5.1 Put the competent cells on ice to melt
3.5.2 Add 5μL DNA to the competent cell suspension, rotate the centrifuge tube gently to mix the contents, and place in ice bath for 30 min.
3.5.3 Place the tube in a 42°C water bath for 60-90 seconds, then quickly transfer to an ice bath for 2-3 minutes, taking care not to shake the tube.
3.5.4 Add 500ul sterile non-resistant LB medium into the centrifuge tube, and shake the culture at 180rpm at 37°C for 1 hour.
3.5.5 Coate 100μL transformed competent cells with LB plate containing chloromycetin and cultured upside down at 37°C for 12-16 hours.
3.6 Yeast transformation
3.6.1 Add 5 μL predenaturated Carrier DNA (YT0003), 50 μL yeast receptor cells, and 350 μL LiAc/PEG3350 mixture (YT0006) into a 1.5 mL sterile centrifuge tube containing about 1 μg plasmid, and mix gently.
3.6.2 Take water bath at 30 ℃ for 30 min, gently mix it upside down every 10 min.
3.6.3 Add 20 μL DMSO to each tube, mix gently upside-down.
3.6.4 42 ℃ water bath heat shock for 15 min, gently mixing every 5 min upside down.
3.6.5 Supernatant was discarded after centrifugation at 3,000 RMP for 5 min.
3.6.6 The thall deposits were suspended again with 1 mL YPD Plus Liquid Medium, and shaken on a 30 ℃ shaker for 30-60 min.
3.6.7 Centrifuged at 3,000 RMP for 5 min, the supernatant was discarded and 1 mL sterile 0.9% NaCl suspension was added.
3.6.8 Diluted 10 times and 100 times, respectively, then coated on the corresponding defective medium plate.
3.6.9 The plate was inverted at 30 ℃ for 3-5 days.
