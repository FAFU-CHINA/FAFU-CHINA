Education and Communication

Overview

Synthetic biology aims to build artificial biosystems that operate like circuits. Using the ideas and tools of synthetic biology, many fantastic ideas are being realized. Synthetic biology is very different from the traditional biological sciences, and we want to educate the society about this new discipline, as well as the general public about smell.

University

For Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University

Due to the influence of COVID-19 's epidemic situation, our team was forced to choose to carry out educational popularization activities in our school. We held an educational seminar, a popular science iGEM competition and synthetic biology.

After the completion of our popular science speech, many students came to ask us questions, and we answered the relevant questions to them one by one. Through this lecture, many students say that they like synthetic biology very much, and many of them are willing to become a member of iGEM or participate in the research of synthetic biology in the future.

For Fuzhou University

When we learned that the team of Fuzhou University (another team in Fuzhou), due to the newly formed team, there are limited, or even basically no, synthetic biology-related science seminars on campus for college students and graduate students. As brother colleges, our two teams got in touch at the end of the spring semester and reached a cooperative relationship. In this year's competition, we will help each other to grow and progress together.

In the initial stage, through several project sharing and exchange meetings and human society practice exchange meetings, we initially agreed to jointly hold a synthetic biology science publicity seminar on the campus of Fuzhou University under the premise of epidemic prevention and control.

This presentation meeting is for the newly enrolled students of Fuzhou University in 2021 and students of all grades who are interested in synthetic biology. This conference has a total of more than 200 people. At the same time, there will be two teachers from the leading group of Fuzhou University. Of course, during this seminar, the students at the scene were active in thinking, actively asking questions and communicating, and the response was enthusiastic. As the organizer of the event, we also distributed corresponding prizes and souvenirs to the students. We will remember every like-minded friend on the 3rd floor of the east of Fuzhou University in September 10, 2021.

We sent our brochures to ZJU-China and CAU-China

High school

We distributed brochures to senior high school teams and cooperated with senior high school teams to carry out popular science propaganda activities on their campuses. We learned that high school students already had a preliminary understanding of genetic engineering, and based on that, we introduced high school students to the concept of synthetic biology. We encourage them to learn about this emerging discipline and participate in iGEM competitions.

Children


FUSHAN park

（The consent of the guardian has been obtained for taking photos that interact with minors.）
On October 3rd, we organized an theme activity of synthetic biology and nature in Fushan National Park for students from the kindergarten affiliated to Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University to get close to nature and konw some knowledge of synthetic biology.
Before the activity began, we conducted a survey of kindergarten students through their parents, and found that most of the students were around 5 years old. They had no knowledge of synthetic biology and had never participated in any activities. Therefore, in order to facilitate kindergarten children to better participate in this activity, we designed a simple and interesting game, children as long as the picture of bacteria and the name on the card match can get a reward. We also invite parents to interact with their children, which will not only deepen parents' understanding/interest in SYNTHETIC biology, but also give students a good introduction to synthetic biology. In addition, we also draw cartoon patterns of neroli and linalool on the back of bookmarks as a reward for children. Children who actively participate in the game will have a chance to win bookmarks, which will greatly increase their interest in synthetic biology.
Through this activity, we shared some simple synthetic biology tips with children to stimulate their interest in biology. In the future, we plan to hold more related activities to introduce more biological knowledge and better promote synthetic biology.

The 21st century is the century of biology. In order to better popularize synthetic biology, we have held an extremely important and meaningful activity for children's scientific enlightenment.

Primary school students

The third primary school of fuzhou education school

On the morning of October 8th, our FAFU-CHINA iGEM team arrived at the third Primary School affiliated to Fuzhou Institute of Education for a popular science lecture. The purpose of this lecture is to enable primary school students to light the lamp of science, enhance their understanding of synthetic biology and create a good atmosphere for learning synthetic biology.
We warmed up the interaction with the short story of two yeasts. At the beginning, we first introduced microorganisms, and the children expressed their own views on this. What is a microbe? Where are the microbes? Does it mean that microbes we can't see don't exist? We explained this one by one. Then we extend viruses, bacteria and fungi through microbes. In the second part, we begin to introduce the uniqueness of our project.Why is it fragrant? Where does the fragrance come from? We summarized the origin of the fragrance from a microscopic point of view, which the children talked about with great delight. In the third part, we gave a brief introduction to our project. We first told a short story, and then we introduced the general steps of our project in ABC order. Finally, we accepted the children's questions and answered their bewilderment.
This activity not only brings scientific interest to the students' classroom, but also makes them love science more.

Fuzhou huaian experimental primary school

Our fAFU-China iGEM team arrived at Huai 'an Experimental Primary School in Fuzhou and gave a popular science lecture. We came to the three (1) class, the three (1) class for the basic science lectures. The popular science lectures are roughly divided into three parts. First, we introduce the topic of biology and what kinds of creatures are included in children's world. Secondly, we and the children on the topic of "fragrance", launched a discussion, the children said that they prefer fresh taste. And then we started introducing them to microbes. We introduced the yeast used in our project by introducing microbes, and made it easier for them to understand our project by explaining the stories of comic characters. Through the question-and-answer session after the explanation, we felt the children's curiosity about the microbial world, and also felt the children's love for science popularization activities.

Science lecture on no-till farm

We cooperated with the wild Growing Nature Creative camp to carry out popular science lectures on the fragrance of microorganisms for families.

Before the activity began, we conducted safety training for participants to protect the personal safety of each child participating in the lecture. Each experience area was protected by our team members, and the microscope experience area and section were protected by trained team members. Every photograph we take is taken with the consent of the subject, protecting the privacy of all who are photographed. We started with the promotional video of FAFU-China IGEM team in 2021, introducing the knowledge related to biology and microbes. Children were full of interest and curiosity about the world of microbes. When the children have a certain understanding of microorganisms, we began to introduce our project in simple and easy to understand language, how to use microorganisms to produce aroma. With the help of our self-made cartoons, the complex experiment process has become a daily life that children can understand. In the course of the lecture, the children actively asked questions, and in the process of answering questions, they deepened their understanding of microorganisms and the production of aroma. We also help the children to make temporary pieces, let the children find their own fragrant plant leaves, petals, they found mint leaves, lemon leaves, we also use onion, pumpkin and other existing materials, and will make good pieces under the microscope, take the children to appreciate the world under the microscope.
